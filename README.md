# LastbencherOS

**LastbencherOS** is a high-performance, tactical academic assistant designed for students to manage their attendance with precision and efficiency. It combines visual analytics with smart scheduling to ensure you stay above your target percentage without missing a beat of student life.

Built with a focus on privacy and speed, all your data remains stored locally on your device.

---

## 🚀 Key Features

### 📊 Tactical Dashboard
*   **Real-time Efficiency Tracking**: Instantly view your current attendance percentage vs. your set goal.
*   **Bunk Counselor**: Smart logic that tells you exactly how many classes you can safely skip or how many you must attend to reach your goal.
*   **Visual Analytics**: Interactive pie charts, line graphs, and bar charts powered by Recharts to visualize your attendance trends over days, weeks, and months.

### 🗓️ Smart Timetable Manager
*   **AI Timetable Extraction**: Upload an image of your class schedule, and the integrated Gemini AI will automatically extract and populate your weekly timetable.
*   **Manual Entry**: Easily add, edit, or remove lecture slots for every day of the week.
*   **Strategic Planning**: Your schedule feeds directly into the attendance tracker for automated daily logging.

### 📅 Attendance Tracker
*   **One-Tap Logging**: Quickly mark subjects as Present, Absent, or Cancelled.
*   **Automatic Weightage**: The system understands your weekly frequency and calculates the impact of every single class on your overall score.
*   **Saturday Overrides**: Handle irregular Saturday schedules with custom subject overrides.

### ⚙️ Identity & Security
*   **Local Node Persistence**: Your data never leaves your browser. It is stored in local storage for maximum privacy and instant access.
*   **Custom Thresholds**: Set your own goal (e.g., 75%, 80%) and watch the system adjust all its recommendations.
